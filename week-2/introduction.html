Vardhaman College of Engineering (VCE), established in 1999, is promoted by the Vardhaman Educational Society, a not-for-profit organization committed to advancing quality education. The Society was founded with a vision to establish and manage institutions that impart education across diverse disciplines, including engineering, technology, sciences, management, and vocational training, while fostering creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurial skills among students. The Society operates on a non-profit and non-commercial basis, focusing on societal development and knowledge dissemination. Under the aegis of the Vardhaman Educational Society, VCE has emerged as a leading institution known for academic excellence, research contributions, and holistic student development. The institution continues to uphold the core objectives of the Society by integrating quality education, innovation, and social responsibility, thereby contributing significantly to the advancement of technical education and societal progress. Vardhaman College of Engineering (VCE), is a premier UGC autonomous institution located in Hyderabad, Telangana, affiliated to JNTUH and approved by AICTE. Accredited by NAAC with an A++ Grade and NBA (Tier-I, Washington Accord), the institution offers a comprehensive range of academic programs including B.Tech, M.Tech, and MBA, catering to a large and diverse student community. With UGC autonomous status valid up to 2030–31 and recognition under Sections 2(f) and 12(B), VCE has consistently demonstrated academic excellence, quality assurance, and institutional credibility. The institution is committed to delivering outcome-based education supported by modern pedagogical practices such as experiential learning, problem-based learning, and participative learning. VCE boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, advanced laboratories, centers of excellence, and a well-equipped digital library. Strong industry collaborations, active MoUs, and a dedicated Career Development Cell (CDC) ensure that students are industry-ready, reflected in consistently high placement outcomes. The institution also promotes innovation and entrepreneurship through its Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CIE), fostering a culture of creativity and start-up development. Vardhaman has established itself as a hub for research and innovation, with significant funding support, extensive publications, and a strong patent portfolio. Faculty and students actively engage in national and international research collaborations with organizations such as ISRO, DRDO, and IISc. The institution is also deeply committed to sustainability, social responsibility, and holistic student development, aligning its initiatives with NEP 2020 and global best practices. Through continuous improvement, global engagement, and community-oriented initiatives, VCE continues to strengthen its position as a leading institution in engineering education and research.
